🎯 Problem & Solution

Problem: Amateur games with friends often feel unstructured — no scores, no standings, no real competition.

Solution: A clean web platform that manages the full league lifecycle in one place, from signup to final standings.

👥 Target Audience

Teens and adults who play basketball, football, or other sports recreationally and want a simple, fun way to keep score and compete with friends.
